Adjaruli Khachapuri (აჭარული ხაჭაპური): A signature dish from Georgia, Adjaruli Khachapuri is a boat-shaped bread filled with melted cheese, topped with a raw egg yolk and a pat of butter. It's traditionally mixed at the table, creating a rich, savory filling that's enjoyed by tearing off pieces of the crust to dip into the cheese mixture.

Pizza: Originating from Italy, pizza is a globally beloved dish consisting of a flatbread base topped with tomato sauce, cheese, and various toppings such as meats, vegetables, and herbs. It has numerous regional variations and styles, from thin-crust Neapolitan to deep-dish Chicago-style.

Shawarma: Shawarma is a Middle Eastern dish made of thinly sliced cuts of meat, like lamb, beef, or chicken, that are stacked and cooked on a vertical rotisserie. The cooked meat is shaved off and typically served in a flatbread such as pita, accompanied by vegetables and sauces like tahini or garlic sauce.

Fries: Also known as French fries, these are batons of deep-fried potatoes, crispy on the outside and soft on the inside. They are a popular side dish and snack worldwide, often seasoned with salt and served with condiments like ketchup or mayonnaise.

Chicken Wings: Chicken wings are a popular appetizer or snack, often deep-fried or baked and then coated in various sauces or seasonings. They can be prepared in numerous styles, such as spicy Buffalo wings or savory barbecue wings, and are commonly served with dipping sauces and sides like celery sticks.
